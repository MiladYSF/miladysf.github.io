About Me

I'm Milad, a cloud and cybersecurity enthusiast with a background in computer science through Harvard's CS50 program. After discovering how interconnected cloud infrastructure and security have become, I shifted my focus toward building practical skills at that intersection — earning foundational certifications in AWS and Azure while currently working toward CompTIA Security+.

I am ambitious and driven to continuously grow as an IT professional. I thrive in environments where learning and collaboration are central, and where there is room to take initiative. I am looking for a place where I can challenge myself and build a long-term career.
